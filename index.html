Serbia Air Quality WebGIS

Mapping air quality, land cover transitions and population exposure using QGIS, GeoServer and OpenLayers.

Open WebMap View Workflow

Project Overview

This project investigates air quality patterns in Serbia by combining CAMS European Air Quality Reanalysis data, ESRI 10m Annual Land Cover maps and WorldPop population counts. The final outputs are published through an interactive WebGIS to support spatial exploration of pollution trends, land cover change and population exposure.

The workflow focuses on three pollutants: NO₂, PM2.5 and PM10. It includes annual mean aggregation change, land cover transition analysis, zonal statistics and bivariate exposure mapping.

Key Outputs

Air Quality

NO₂, PM2.5 and PM10 AMAC maps, concentration maps and zonal statistics.

Land Cover

Serbia land cover transition map between 2021 and 2023.

Exposure

Bivariate maps combining population class and pollutant severity class.
